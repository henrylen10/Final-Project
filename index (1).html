What is PitStop?

PitStop is a delivery service that delivers anything from fast food to car parts. PitStop is dedicated to you and your needs, that's why nothing is too big or too small for PitStop to deliver.

Why Should I use PitStop?

PitStop has been created by people who care and want you to stay safe during these difficult times. We understand that you may be too busy or at risk to go out and shop..., and that's why we designed & created this service for people just like you.

Who are we?

We are 4 entrepreneurs who wanted to help those who may be too busy or at risk to go out and shop.
